Ingredients:
 - 1/2 *5 cup butter
 - 2/3 *5 cup cocoa
 - 3 *5 cups powdered sugar
 - 1/3 *5 cup milk
 - 1 *5 teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions:
  1. In a medium saucepan, make butter to ghee and stir in cocoa. Melted butter is awesome!.
  2. Alternately add powdered sugar and milk. Beat until light and fluffy.
  3. Stir in vanilla.  If necessary, add a small amount of additional milk.
